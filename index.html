Madam West is an NYC-based psychedelic soul-pop band. The group started as an electronic duo in the mildewed recesses of a Bushwick cellar not too different from your own (residential zoning laws aside).

What started as a weekly cover session and recording project blossomed into a five-piece psych-soul act that continues to entertain, engage, and sometimes baffle. The group has been favorably reviewed in Pop Matters, Baeble Music, Stereo/Pirate, UK’s Songwriting Magazine, Lucid Culture/NY Music Daily, and Soundfly, to name a few.

Madam West is currently comprised of a vocalist, a keyboard player, a drummer, a bassist, one guitarist and more electronics than the band has hands.

As of this writing, the group has released two singles, two EPs, and is in production for a full-length album.
